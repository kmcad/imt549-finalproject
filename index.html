Around the World in Five Family Kitchens

Recipes from the Becker, Cadena, Costello, Lee, and Wong families

This cookbook has been years in the making. What started out as a “simple” project of gathering all of our various families’ recipes turned into a fun but labor-intensive journey through the stories and histories we’ve all shared at the dining room table. Over the course of about 24 months, I have made all but a handful of the recipes in this cookbook. At first, I wanted to make sure I had pictures of every dish because I hate opening a cookbook and finding a wall of text, but no image of what the finished product will look like. Even with something that is straightforward, I think pictures make the recipe infinitely more enticing. As I kept going, I found that making each recipe made me better understand the possible challenges each might present. I’d like to share the stories, pictures, and recipes with all of you in a virtual index that connects the dishes to the family members who contributed them, almost like a virtual potluck.
